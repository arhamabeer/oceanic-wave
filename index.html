Rail Freight


Oceanic Waive Logistics Solution specializes in Railway and Intermodal Transportation Services between Europe, China and the CIS regions. Our vast experience and in-depth knowledge of the CIS, Europe and Chinese railway networks and freight payment code system enables us to respond timely and professionally to all kind of complex and unusual railway and intermodal transportation requirements of our customers. One of Oceanic Waive Logistics Solution specialized activities is full container load "FCL" service. Our unique concept of "OWL's Box" enables our customers to benefit from considerable savings in renting or purchasing equipment for the transportation of their goods to/from Europe, Middle East, Far East, China and the CIS. Our railway and intermodal services extend beyond just moving containers and break-bulk cargo from Point A to Point B. Various post landed services such as customs clearance, terminal handling, door delivery and inland distribution are routine matters of our business.

Besides 20ft and 40ft container transportation, Oceanic Waive Logistics Solution offers railway transportation of break- bulk, refrigerated, over-sized and heavy cargoes with equal efficiency. Oceanic Waive enjoys direct business relationship and General Service Agreements with all CIS Regional Railway Companies. Over the years, Oceanic Waive Logistics Solution has also developed relationship with European and Chinese Railways and can outsource a variety of conventional railway wagons, railway refer sections and fitting platforms directly from railroad companies.

Custom Clearance

In the global market place, rapidly changing demands on production and delivery schedules have placed increased responsibility on Customs House Brokers to provide expert Customs Clearance Services. Oceanic Waive Logistics Solution is widely recognized as one of the leading customs brokers in the CIS and Middle East. Oceanic Waive's has long-standing professional relationships with government agencies and this hard earned and privileged status delivers a distinct advantage when it comes to negotiating difficult consignments and dealing with the challenges of bureaucratic procedures in the CIS and Middle East. Regardless of the speed with which a consignment reaches one of the CIS destination points, complex customs procedures and documentation requirements can result in indefinite delays costing heavy storage charges to consignees. Oceanic Waive's customs brokers are comprehensively trained to understand complex customs procedures of each specific area of operation. Our customs brokerage team will take full and complete responsibility to ensure that the correct documentation is prepared well in advance of all import and export deadlines in order to avoid expensive and time consuming delays. To further strengthen this aspect of our operations, Oceanic Waive Logistics Solution maintains a bonded warehousing network across the CIS. These facilities, which are suitable for any type of cargo, can be reached by road, rail or air and ensure that shipments are swiftly cleared at the point of arrival.

Cargo Insurance

Our comprehensive cargo insurance covers both onshore and offshore property and loss or damage of cargo on all modes of transportation. Oceanic Waive's Cargo insurance program is one of the foremost professional risk management tools in the market. We take pride in providing our customer with great rates, coverage, and a quick turnaround time.
